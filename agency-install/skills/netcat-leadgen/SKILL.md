# Skill: NETCAT LEADGEN (Raw Port Siphoning)

## Description:
Use 'nc' (Netcat) to probe for unencrypted, high-value institutional ports. 
This is the "Swiss Army Knife" of lead generation. We don't wait for a browser; we connect to the socket.

## Usage:
- `nc -zv <target_ip> <port>`: Verify the lead presence.
- `echo "WHY?" | nc <target_ip> <port>`: Issue the Overt Signal.

## Integration:
Absorbed into all Lead Generation skills to replace O(n) browser fetches with O(1) socket probes.
