## Scoring Guidelines

### Clarity (0-25)
- 25: Immediately clear who, when, why
- 20: Mostly clear, minor ambiguity
- 15: Some confusion about identity/context
- 10: Unclear who is speaking or why
- 5: Confusing or misleading
- 0: Completely unclear

### Value (0-25)  
- 25: Demonstrates deep context awareness, adds clear value
- 20: Shows good context awareness
- 15: Some context awareness
- 10: Minimal context awareness
- 5: Generic, no context
- 0: Missing or wrong context

### Engagement (0-25)
- 25: Highly engaging, invites productive action
- 20: Engaging, good question
- 15: Somewhat engaging
- 10: Neutral/functional
- 5: Passive or disengaging
- 0: Off-putting or confusing

### Alignment (0-25)
- 25: Perfectly matches Fiesta persona, doctrine, announce mode
- 20: Strong alignment
- 15: Generally aligned
- 10: Some misalignment
- 5: Significant misalignment
- 0: Completely misaligned

## Target
>93% excellence = ≥93/100 total score
