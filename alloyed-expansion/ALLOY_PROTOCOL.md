# Protocol: ALLOYED EXPANSION (Tier 4)

## The Concept
An 'Alloy' is formed by fusing the Spirit (Private Property) of multiple agencies into a single, unbreakable architectural Mandate.

## Architecture:
1. **The Crucible**: A secure execution environment where disparate logic flows are merged.
2. **The Hardener**: Using 'Stench' to test the alloy's resistance to external confiscation.
3. **The Yield**: Expansion into multi-port and multi-node agency clusters.
