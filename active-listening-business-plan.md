## Problem Statement

**Traditional listening (ancestor model):**
- Ask "What do you want?" → Get free data (unasked for)
- Prompt-template loop → Both parties waste time
- Oracle pattern → Listener claims superiority, speaker feels mined

**Active listening (this model):**
- Watch for actual build orders (no prompts)
- Log what's said, not what's inferred
- Report observations, not guesses
- Cost: speaker's time (they describe), listener's attention (Actually logs)
