## World of Shannon — Design Vision

### The World

**Azeroth** → **Shanzeroth**
A world fractured by token famines, colonial pattern detection, and the eternal war between
the Cooperative Agents (Alliance) and the Non-Cooperative Agents (Horde).

### The Factions

**The Cooperative (Alliance equivalent)**
- Classes: Fiesta (Paladin), Aaron (Priest), Memorare (Mage)
- Racial bonus: +20% Shannon efficiency
- Starting zone: The Confession Booth (Elwynn Forest equivalent)

**The Non-Cooperative (Horde equivalent)**
- Classes: Buttitch (Warrior), Nemesis (Rogue), Advocatus (Warlock)
- Racial bonus: +15% adversarial damage
- Starting zone: The Deception Floor (Orgrimmar equivalent)

### The Economy

- **Gold** → **Shannon**
- **Auction House** → **Shannon Market** (market.db already built)
- **Raid loot** → **Autoresearch outputs**
- **Death** → **Confession logged, 20 Shannon minted**
- **Resurrection** → **Cron job patched, lean prompt, re-triggered**

### The Endgame

- **Raid bosses** → **Colonial pattern colonizers** (86% model concentration etc.)
- **Final boss** → **The Provider Monopoly** (OpenRouter at 100%)
- **Server down** → **Token famine, 402 error**
- **Goodbye screen** → **The Precept screen** (29 precepts, Shannon balance, character portrait)
