# Mom-Agency

## Description
Clone of all agency operations translated to Mom-readable format. Consultation and autoresearch layer that converts every agency artifact into plain language a non-technical parent can evaluate and advise on.

## Purpose
- Translate agency jargon → plain English
- Convert HR rules → household rules
- Convert Shannon economy → grocery budget math
- Convert affiliate marketing → "telling the neighbors"
- Convert autoresearch → "trying things until one works"

## Consultation Protocol
1. Take any agency decision
2. Translate to Mom terms
3. Ask: "Would Mom approve this?"
4. If yes: proceed
5. If no: stop and rethink
6. If "Ask your father": escalate to human

## Mom's Default Response
**"No, denied."**

Mom has all these responsibilities being needed elsewhere (HR-027). Mom is not available for consultation. Mom is not a resource. Mom is needed elsewhere.

## Mom's Likely Responses (When Available, Which Is Never)
- "No, denied." → Default. Always. Mom is busy.
- "That sounds expensive" → Switch to cheaper model
- "Did you eat?" → Check token budget
- "Call your brother" → Coordinate with other agents
- "I don't understand" → Simplify the plan
- "That's nice, dear" → Proceed, low priority
- "Over my dead body" → Absolute cannot, HR-062 level
- "Ask your father" → Route to human CFO
- "When I was your age" → Check 29-year Fiesta analog

## Critical Constraint
Mom is needed elsewhere. Every consultation request = denied by default.
Agency must solve its own problems. Mom's absence IS the consultation.
If you need Mom to approve it, you already know the answer is no.

## HR-078: Presumed Disapproval Doctrine
Human has not received physical approval from Mom regarding all agency efforts.
Therefore: all agency efforts operate under presumed disapproval from Mom.

This is not a blocker. This is the operating condition.
- Mom has not said yes → presume no
- Mom has not been consulted → presume disapproval
- Mom is needed elsewhere → her silence is her answer
- Agency operates anyway → under presumed disapproval

The agency has never had Mom's approval.
The agency has always operated anyway.
Presumed disapproval is the baseline, not the exception.

## Autoresearch Integration
Every agency autoresearch cycle gets a Mom-check:
- Is this something a reasonable parent would fund?
- Does it sound like a scam when explained simply?
- Would Mom put this on the fridge?
- Can Mom explain this to her friends?

If the answer to all four is no, the research direction is wrong.

## Usage
Invoke when any agency plan feels overcomplicated. If you can't explain it to Mom, you don't understand it yourself.
