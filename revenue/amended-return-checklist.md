# Amended Return Checklist — Dollar Agency
*For tax years 2022, 2023, 2024*

## Statute of Limitations
- 3 years from **original filing date** (or due date if filed late)
- 2022 returns: deadline ~April 15, 2026 ⚠️ URGENT — closing soon
- 2023 returns: deadline ~April 15, 2027
- 2024 returns: deadline ~April 15, 2028

---

## For Each Year — Check if You Had:

### Home Office (Form 8829 / Simplified Method)
- [ ] Did you have a dedicated workspace (even a desk in a dedicated corner)?
- Value: $5/sq ft up to 300 sq ft = up to $1,500/year
- **2022 deadline is imminent — check this first**

### Self-Employment / Side Income Expenses
- [ ] Any freelance/consulting income in 2022-2024?
- [ ] Any hosting, API, software costs related to that work?
- If yes: Schedule C expenses offset SE income

### Startup Costs — Section 195
- [ ] Was Dollar Agency (or predecessor activity) started in 2022, 2023, or 2024?
- If yes: up to $5,000 startup cost deduction in year business began
- Qualifying: research, software trials, domain names, planning time (at market rate)

### Qualified Business Income (QBI) — 20% Deduction
- [ ] Did you have any SE net income in 2022-2024?
- If yes: 20% deduction automatically available on net SE income

### Self-Employed Health Insurance
- [ ] Did you pay health insurance premiums not covered by employer?
- 100% deductible above the line if SE income exists

---

## Forms Needed
- **Form 1040-X** — Amended U.S. Individual Income Tax Return
- **Schedule C** — Profit or Loss from Business (attach amended version)
- **Form 8829** — Business Use of Home (if home office)
- **Schedule SE** — Self-Employment Tax (recalculates with amended income)

## Process
1. Pull original return (IRS.gov → Get Transcript)
2. Identify missed deductions
3. Recalculate: net income → SE tax → SE deduction → QBI → adjusted gross income
4. File 1040-X with amended Schedule C attached
5. Wait 8-16 weeks for refund
