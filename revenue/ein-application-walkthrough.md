## Step-by-Step

**Step 1:** Go to the URL above. Click **"Apply Online Now"**

**Step 2:** Read the disclaimer. Click **"Begin Application"**

**Step 3:** Entity type → Select **"Sole Proprietor"**
*(Even if you plan to form an LLC later, apply as sole proprietor now — you can get a separate EIN for the LLC when it's formed)*

**Step 4:** Reason → Select **"Started a new business"**

**Step 5:** Enter your information:
- Legal first name, middle initial, last name
- SSN
- Trade name / DBA: **Dollar Agency**
- Business address: [your NYC address]

**Step 6:** Principal business activity:
- Category: **Professional, Scientific, and Technical Services**
- Specific activity: **Computer Programming, Data Processing**

**Step 7:** Start date: **March 2026**

**Step 8:** Review all entries. Click **"Submit"**

**Step 9:** 🎉 EIN issued **immediately on screen**

**Step 10:** ⚠️ **PRINT OR SAVE THE CONFIRMATION PAGE NOW**
This is your CP575 EIN Assignment Notice. The IRS will not re-send it. If you lose it, you must call the IRS Business & Specialty Tax Line (800-829-4933) to retrieve it — that's a 45-minute wait on a good day.
