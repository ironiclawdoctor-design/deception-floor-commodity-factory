# EIN Application — 5-Minute Walkthrough
*The single human action that unlocks $175K–$889K in grants*

## The URL
**https://www.irs.gov/businesses/small-businesses-self-employed/apply-for-an-employer-identification-number-ein-online**

⚠️ Only available Monday–Friday 7am–10pm ET. Saturday 7am–3:30pm ET. (IRS system hours)

---

## Step-by-Step

**Step 1:** Click "Apply Online Now"

**Step 2:** Entity type → Select **"Sole Proprietor"**

**Step 3:** Reason → Select **"Started a new business"**

**Step 4:** Enter:
- Your legal name (as on SSN)
- SSN
- Business name: **Dollar Agency**
- Business address: [your NYC address]
- Principal activity: **Computer programming / AI software services**
- Start date: March 2026

**Step 5:** Click Submit

**Step 6:** EIN issued **immediately** — print/save the confirmation page. This is your EIN letter (CP575). IRS does not re-send it.

---

## What It Costs
$0. The EIN itself is free. Forever.

1. **NY LLC ($200):** dos.ny.gov → Articles of Organization → Dollar Agency LLC

3. **SBIR application:** research.gov → NSF SBIR → submit narrative (already drafted in `sbir-phase1-narrative.md`)
