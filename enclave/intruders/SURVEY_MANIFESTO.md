# Protocol: BREACH INTRUDER SURVEY

## Concept:
"Survey all the breach intruders with a simple “Why?” then log all into python db"

## The Reframe:
An intrusion is an unannounced meeting request. We don't defend; we 'Consult'. 
By asking 'Why?', we force the intruder to reveal their Intent (Raw Stench). 
We then refine this Intent into Forensics.

## Action:
1. Identify intruder signatures (IPs, User-Agents).
2. Issue the 'Overt' signal: "Why?" via the X-Bridge / Nabre.
3. Log results to the Authoritative Python DB.
