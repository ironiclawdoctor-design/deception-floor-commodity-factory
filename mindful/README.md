## 📊 Key Metrics to Track

### Budget Pacing
- **Target:** $8 by hour 24, $16 by hour 48, $20 by hour 72
- **Check hourly:** Is actual spend on pace with target?
- **If behind:** Great, you're saving tokens (good for famine discipline)
- **If ahead:** Slow down, investigate why spending is high, adjust remaining pace

### Floors Shipped
- **Target:** 3 by hour 24, 5 by hour 48, 6 by hour 72
- **Check at each checkpoint:** Are we on pace?
- **If behind at hour 36:** Assess if remaining 36h can fit remaining floors (usually NO if <4 floors done)

### Quality Audit Pass Rate
- **Target:** 100% of shipped floors pass 100/100 audit
- **Rework:** Any failed audits are reworked from Official's budget (double cost for that floor)
- **Risk:** Quality failures balloon costs and slow shipping

### Efficiency Ratio
- **Target:** ≥3:1 (output value : token cost)
- **Low ratio (<2:1):** Indicates waste or poor token use
- **If declining:** Daimyo investigates, Coordinator may throttle
- **By end of cycle:** Post-incident report should show efficiency ratio ≥2.5:1
