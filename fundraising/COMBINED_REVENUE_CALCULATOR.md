## Scenario 1: Conservative Model (Year 1)

*Realistic projections based on small user base and modest adoption.*

### Donation Revenue

**Assumptions:**
- 500 active users
- 5% conversion rate to donors
- $25 average donation

**Math:**
- Donors: 500 × 0.05 = 25 people
- Monthly donations: 25 × $25 = $625/month
- **Annual:** $625 × 12 = **$7,500**

### Subscription Revenue

**Assumptions:**
- 100 users convert to paid tier
- $10/month per subscriber
- 90% retention

**Math:**
- Monthly: 100 × $10 = $1,000
- **Annual (with churn):** $10,000 × 0.95 = **$9,500**

### Stripe Affiliate Revenue

**Assumptions:**
- Moderate promotion of Stripe links
- 20 referral sign-ups per month
- Average merchant processes $5,000/month
- Stripe fee: ~2.7% = $135/merchant/month
- Commission: 25% Year 1

**Math:**
- Monthly commission per referral: $135 × 0.25 = $33.75
- New referrals this month: 20 × $33.75 = $675
- Cumulative from prior months: $675 (month 1), $1,350 (month 2), etc.
- Growth: ~$33.75 × month × 20 new referrals = accelerating
- **Month 1:** $675
- **Month 6:** ~$4,000
- **Month 12:** ~$7,000
- **Year 1 Total:** ~$40,000 (conservative, growing exponentially)

### **YEAR 1 TOTAL: ~$57,000**

```
Conservative Model (Year 1)
─────────────────────────────────
Donations:         $7,500 (steady)
Subscriptions:     $9,500 (steady, some churn)
Stripe Affiliate: $40,000 (accelerating growth)
─────────────────────────────────
TOTAL:            $57,000
```

**Breakdown by Quarter:**
- Q1: ~$8,000 (ramp-up)
- Q2: ~$12,000 (growing affiliate)
- Q3: ~$15,000 (network effect)
- Q4: ~$22,000 (holiday donations + affiliate base)

## Scenario 2: Optimized Model (Year 1+)

*More aggressive promotion and conversion optimization.*

### Donation Revenue

**Assumptions:**
- 2,000 active users (4x growth)
- 8% conversion (better messaging)
- $40 average (higher-intent donors)

**Math:**
- Donors: 2,000 × 0.08 = 160 people
- Monthly: 160 × $40 = $6,400
- **Annual:** $6,400 × 12 = **$76,800**

### Subscription Revenue

**Assumptions:**
- 400 paid subscribers (4x growth)
- $15/month per subscriber (premium tier)
- 90% retention

**Math:**
- Monthly: 400 × $15 = $6,000
- **Annual:** $6,000 × 12 × 0.95 = **$68,400**

### Stripe Affiliate Revenue

**Assumptions:**
- Dedicated landing page + email campaign + social
- 80 referral sign-ups per month
- Average merchant processes $8,000/month (better targeting)
- Stripe fee: ~2.7% = $216/merchant/month
- Commission: 25% Year 1, then 12% ongoing (Year 2+)

**Math:**
- New referrals: 80/month
- Commission per referral: $216 × 0.25 = $54
- Month 1-12 growth: 80 × $54 × 12 months (avg) = growing pool
- Exponential accrual:
  - Month 1: 80 × $54 = $4,320
  - Month 6: (80×6) × $54 = ~$25,920
  - Month 12: (80×12) × $54 = ~$51,840
- **Year 1 Total:** ~$270,000

**Breakdown by Quarter:**

**Key Difference:** Dedicated Stripe affiliate marketing (landing page, emails, social) drives 4x referral volume.

## Key Assumptions & Sensitivity Analysis

### What Could Change?

**Upside Risks (Revenue Higher):**
- 🚀 Fiesta user base grows faster than 4x
- 🚀 Stripe referral conversion improves (current: 7-9%)
- 🚀 More merchants referred sign up for premium Stripe products
- 🚀 Network effects increase donation/subscription willingness

**Downside Risks (Revenue Lower):**
- 📉 User acquisition slows
- 📉 Stripe commission rates drop (possible but unlikely)
- 📉 Referral quality declines (less actionable leads)
- 📉 Market saturation in affiliate space

**Impact:** Stripe referrals have the highest sensitivity (9-10% swing). Focus optimization here.

## Transparency & Disclosure Requirements

### By Revenue Stream

**Donations:**
- No special disclosure needed
- Optional: Thank donors, show impact

**Subscriptions:**
- Clear pricing pages
- No hidden fees
- Cancellation process easy

**Stripe Affiliate:**
- ✅ **Mandatory:** Disclose at every link point
- ✅ **Language:** "We earn a commission if you sign up via this link at no cost to you"
- ✅ **Placement:** Near the CTA, not hidden
- ✅ **Consistency:** Same wording across all channels

### Recommended Disclosure Statement

```markdown
### How We Stay Transparent

We earn revenue in three ways:

1. **Direct Donations** — Gifts from users who believe in our work
2. **Subscriptions** — Optional premium features for power users
3. **Affiliate Commissions** — We earn 25-30% commission if you sign up 
   for Stripe via our link (at no extra cost to you — Stripe's pricing is identical)

**Why Stripe?** We use Stripe ourselves for Fiesta's payment processing. 
It's genuinely excellent, so we recommend it. The commission helps us keep 
building and don't feel bad — that's how affiliate programs work everywhere.

All three revenue streams are transparent and disclosed. Thanks for supporting us! 💙
```

1. **Aligned Incentives** — Users actually benefit from Stripe (same tool we use)

6. **Ethical** — Win-win: users get great tool, Fiesta gets commission
