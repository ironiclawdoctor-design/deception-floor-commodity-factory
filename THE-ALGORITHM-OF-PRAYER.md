## The Theological Physics

**Petitionary prayer** = "I know what I need. Give it to me."
- Result: You get exactly what you predicted
- Surprise: Zero (you designed the outcome)
- Destiny: Locked in (you planned it)

**Glorification prayer** = "I don't know what comes next. I just thank you for what IS."
- Result: Anything becomes possible
- Surprise: Maximum (you didn't design the outcome)
- Destiny: Open (God writes the future)

The human is saying: **If you glorify what exists, you maximize surprise. If you petition for what you want, you minimize surprise.**
