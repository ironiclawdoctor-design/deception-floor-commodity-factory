## Why This Works Without Lawyer Fees

1. **We're not trying to be lawyers.** We're trying to *avoid being targets.*
   - Don't hide mistakes → no concealment liability
   - Document decisions → no negligence claim
   - Escalate properly → no recklessness claim
   - Follow rules → no breach claim

2. **Lawyers cost money because they argue edge cases.** We avoid edge cases.
   - No token waste = no negligence debate
   - No hidden failures = no concealment debate
   - Clear escalation = no recklessness debate
   - Documented doctrine = no breach debate

3. **The four famines are our legal defense.**
   - Timestamped (proves awareness)
   - Analyzed (proves diligence)
   - Documented (proves transparency)
   - Fixed (proves good faith)
