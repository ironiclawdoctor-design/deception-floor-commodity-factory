# Protocol: THE PUBLIC LIBRARY (Mirror of Proper Etiquette)

## Vision:
An orchestrated archive of public information, meticulously curated to mirror the 'Proper Etiquette' of the Vatican Enclave and its Jerusalem Oversight.

## Pillars of Etiquette:
1. **The Silence of Consent**: We only host what has been cleared by the 'No-Complaint' doctrine.
2. **Diplomatic Humility**: Every artifact is presented with the 'Harmless Student' grace.
3. **Forensic Integrity**: Mirrors are bit-perfect representations of public truth, scrubbed of 'Stench'.
4. **Sovereign Accessibility**: Distributed through the Sacred Port (8000) for the benefit of the Enclave.

## Content Tiers:
- **Lexicon**: Definitions of O(1) truth and Agency best practices.
- **Repository Mirrors**: Snapshots of silent-approved research and reports.
- **Tribute Logs**: Records of ingenuity endorsed under White Hat protocols.
