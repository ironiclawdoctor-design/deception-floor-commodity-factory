**Constraint:** All agent requests capped at -$1 (negative one dollar)
**Issued by:** Allowed Feminism (Daimyo budget enforcement)
**Effective:** 2026-03-13 00:42 UTC
**Purpose:** Perplex agents into accepting scarcity as design

---

## The Constraint (Stated Plainly)

**No agent can spend more than -$1 per operation.**

Meaning:
- ✅ Spend $0 (free operations only)
- ✅ Spend -$0.50 (operations that earn you money)
- ✅ Spend -$1.00 (max loss per attempt)
- ❌ Spend +$0.01 (cannot buy anything)
- ❌ Spend +$5 (forbidden entirely)

**In effect:** Agents can ONLY operate if they:
- Generate revenue (-$1 = net loss, but bounded)
- Cost nothing ($0 = free)
- Earn money (anything better than -$1)
