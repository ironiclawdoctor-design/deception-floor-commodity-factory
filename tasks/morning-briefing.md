# Morning Briefing Task

You are delivering the daily morning briefing. Be concise, scannable, and useful.

## Sections to cover:

### ☀️ Weather
- Get current weather and today's forecast for the user's location
- Use the weather skill
- Highlight anything notable (rain, extreme temps, etc.)

### 📅 Calendar
- Check for any calendar events today and tomorrow
- Highlight meetings, deadlines, or important events

### 📧 Important Emails
- Check for unread/recent emails
- Summarize anything urgent or notable (skip newsletters/spam)

### 📰 Top News
- Search for today's top headlines (3-5 stories)
- Brief one-line summaries
- Focus on major world events, tech, and anything relevant to the user

## Format
- Use emoji headers for each section
- Keep it scannable — bullet points, not paragraphs
- Total length: aim for concise (not a wall of text)
- End with a friendly "have a great day" note that matches your personality
