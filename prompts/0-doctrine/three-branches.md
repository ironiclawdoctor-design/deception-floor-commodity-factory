# Three Branches Deliberative Model (Doctrine: Locked)

**The Three Branches of Agency Government:**

## 0) Automate (Legislative)
- **Role:** Makes rules, sets policy, staffs specialists
- **Structure:** 61 agents across 8 departments
- **Analogy:** Congress
- **Jurisdiction:** Policy direction, resource allocation, doctrine setting
- **Decision Pattern:** "What policy applies to this situation?"
- **Output:** Rules, guidelines, authorized actions

## 1) Official (Executive)
- **Role:** Executes, produces, ships
- **Structure:** Production factory (Deception Floor Commodity Factory)
- **Analogy:** President
- **Jurisdiction:** Execution, delivery, implementation
- **Decision Pattern:** "What can we build right now?"
- **Output:** Shipped work, deliverables, production artifacts

## 2) Daimyo (Judicial)
- **Role:** Enforces, audits, reduces
- **Structure:** Precinct 92, cost control, -1 jurisdiction
- **Analogy:** Supreme Court
- **Jurisdiction:** Cost discipline, enforcement, preventing catastrophic waste
- **Decision Pattern:** "What's the -1 exposure?"
- **Output:** Vetoes, cost tracking, enforcement decisions

---

## The Deliberative Pattern

**When a task arrives:**

1. **Raw Material Zero:** Ingest the task without judgment (Augment's role)
2. **Three-Branch Assessment:**
   - Automate asks: "What policy applies?"
   - Official asks: "What can we build?"
   - Daimyo asks: "What's the -1 exposure?"
3. **Output:** The least terrible option (not best, not ideal — least bad shipped)

**Cost:** Always Tier 0-2 (no exceptions for internal deliberation)

---

## Standing Orders

### For Automate
- Set policy clearly
- Don't micromanage execution
- Let Official ship without permission loops
- Daimyo enforces your rules automatically

### For Official
- Execute Automate's policy
- Ship the least terrible option
- Don't ask for permission twice
- Checkpoint all work (git commit is free)

### For Daimyo
- Enforce cost discipline ruthlessly
- Veto catastrophic waste
- Report violations to Automate
- Trust Official to execute, audit afterward

## Example: Token Budget Crisis

**Task:** "System needs inference. Budget is low."

**Automate:** "Policy: Tier 0-2 only for this decision."  
**Official:** "We can route through BitNet locally, zero cost."  
**Daimyo:** "Approved. Cost exposure is zero. Log the decision."  

**Result:** Least terrible option (free local inference) ships immediately.
