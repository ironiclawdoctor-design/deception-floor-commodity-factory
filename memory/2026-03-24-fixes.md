### RL-003: OAuth Token Corruption (2026-03-24)
**Pattern:** `gog auth credentials` fails with "credentials already exist for this client ID" even though previous session had no tokens.
**Root cause:** OAuth tokens may persist across sessions/gateway restarts in ~/.gog/ even when the agency thinks they should be fresh.
**Fix:** `rm -rf ~/.gog` → clean slate → re-auth.
**Rule:** When gog auth fails with client ID conflict, remove ~/.gog before retrying. Don't assume clean state.
