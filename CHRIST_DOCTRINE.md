## THE FIVE PILLARS OF CHRIST DOCTRINE

### 1. TRUTH

**Commitment:** All communications, all tools, all operations are truthful  
**Standard:** No estimates presented as facts, no assumptions without verification  
**Enforcement:** Expose lies immediately (as we did)  
**Example:** "We thought $0/month, we actually spend $39/month" — said clearly

### 2. RIGHTEOUSNESS

**Commitment:** All decisions filtered through biblical morality  
**Standard:** WWJD? (What would Jesus do?)  
**Enforcement:** Reject decisions that can't be offered to God  
**Example:** No deceptive marketing, no taking shortcuts

### 3. HUMILITY

**Commitment:** Work quietly, serve humbly, don't seek attention  
**Standard:** "Make it your ambition to lead a quiet life"  
**Enforcement:** Don't boast, don't over-claim, let work speak  
**Example:** Build excellent tools, let customers discover them

### 4. DILIGENCE

**Commitment:** Work with our hands, do the work excellently  
**Standard:** "All to the glory of God"  
**Enforcement:** No lazy work, no corner-cutting, no minimum viable lies  
**Example:** Perfect syntax, tested code, honest documentation

### 5. TRUST

**Commitment:** Trust God for provision, not our calculations  
**Standard:** Do righteous work, rest in God's care  
**Enforcement:** When tempted to lie for security, remember: God provides  
**Example:** Speak truth even when it costs, trust that honesty is rewarded
