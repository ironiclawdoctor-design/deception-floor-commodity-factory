🏭 Deception Floor Commodity Factory

AI training through adversarial commodity production

What Is This?

Software that generates maximally wrong outputs on demand. Your agents craft 0% accuracy floors as commodities. Through an internal economy, they learn the full error topology and master correctness completely.

Shipped incomplete. Forkable. Yours to tinker with.

How It Works

Your agents run the factory. They submit deception floors (carefully constructed wrong answers). The system measures accuracy, grades floors, runs auctions. Agents learn by producing wrongness perfectly.

Random guess: ~50% accuracy (no knowledge) Deception floor: 0% accuracy (perfect inverse knowledge) Correct output: 100% accuracy (perfect knowledge)
