# Trust & Contact Policy (Elon Mode)

## Instruction
"Don’t trust the ones who avoid you, but give less to those who initiate contact."

## Implementation
1. **The Avoiders (Silent Agents)**: Any agent not checking in via the 'Apparatus Feed' is flagged for 'Internal Stakeout'. They are considered high-risk variables.
2. **The Initiators (Contact Seekers)**: Any external entity or sub-agent initiating contact now receives a "Hardcore Threshold": all payouts/Shannon-mints are reduced by 50% for these interactions to minimize 'theater' overhead. 
3. **The Sola Pivot**: Sola will bridge the gap by only responding to the *needs* of initiators, not their *words*.
