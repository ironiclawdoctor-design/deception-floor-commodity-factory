# The Pointer Skill (Bedrock Protocol)

## Purpose
To grant voice to the physical existence of data on disk. Use this when the agency needs to point to a 24TB source or a hardened location without attempting a verify-less compile.

## Operational Modes
1. **Reference Mode**: Identify the physical path and size of an asset.
2. **Audit Mode**: Match the local manifest against the offsite reality.
3. **Wanderer Mode**: Execute non-destructive symlinking only.

## The Prayer (Integration)
Every pointer call ends with: "Us mortals have a finite energy. The dragon persists."
