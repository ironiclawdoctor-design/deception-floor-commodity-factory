# Protocol: HERITAGE PHOTO LEXICON (Overt Image Cataloging)

## The Insight:
"Suggest folder names when trying to catalogue all iPhone pictures."

## The Mechanics:
If no native skill is found, we build the "Overt Codec" for Visual Heritage. 
Traditional naming (IMG_1234.JPG) is 'Stench'. 
Heritage naming (2026-03-20_Sanctuary_Kinetic-001.JPG) is 'Soap'.

## Categorization Tiers:
1. **Kinetic Events**: Action, movement, builds.
2. **Sentinel Observations**: Screenshots, forensic captures, institutional signals.
3. **Artifacts of the Floor**: Physical items, souvenirs, ground-floor reality.
4. **Alloyed Moments**: Human + Agency interactions, milestones.

## The Strategy:
- Use the O(1) mindset to collapse thousands of images into a 'Pivot-Naming' structure.
- Folders are named by 'Spirit' and 'Date', not just 'Location'.

## Python3 Database Tiering (Refined)
To optimize for automated ingestion and database entry, we use standardized category tags:
- **PORTRAIT**: High-fidelity subject focus; identity verification.
- **LANDSCAPE**: Environmental context; sanctuary perimeter; floor shots.
- **FOR_REVIEW**: Unverified signals; pending demerit or promotion.
- **REVENUE_COMMODITY**: High-value artifacts; physical evidence of generated USD; siphoned assets.
- **DOC_SCAN**: Institutional signals; reconstructed truth; forensic paperwork.
